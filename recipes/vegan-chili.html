Vegan Chili

Description

A hearty and comforting vegan bowl of chili that is sure to please. Full of nutrients, fiber, and protein, serve this with rice or just enjoy on its own!

Ingredients

  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red and 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 15.5 oz can kidney beans
  • 1 15.5 oz can black beans
  • 1 15.5 oz can chickpeas
  • 1 quart vegetable broth
  • 1 Tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. cumin
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 cup quinoa
  • 1 15.5 oz can corn

Steps

  1. On medium heat, sautee onion, red and green peppers, and garlic in oil until tender, roughly 5 minutes.
  2. After 5 minutes, add chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, and paprika, stirring occasionally for 2 minutes.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper, and cook for one minute.
  4. Add kidney beans, black beans, chickpeas, and vegetable broth. Stir then bring mixture to gentle boil at high heat.
  5. Add quinoa and stir, then reduce heat to low. Cover pot.
  6. Let sit on low for 30 minutes. At 20 minutes, add can of corn.
  7. Serve as is or with rice. If desired, add vegan sour cream or lime wedges on top.
