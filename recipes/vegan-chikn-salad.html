Vegan Chikn Salad

Description

This is a vegan take on the chicken salad sandwich. Packed with protein and fiber, chikn salad can be served on bread as a sandwich, but is absolutely delicious eaten on crackers or by itself!

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayonaise
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 2 pieces bread (if serving as sandwich)
  • 2 thin slices red onion (if serving as a sandwich)
  • 2 leaves of Romaine lettuce or handful of favorite greens (I like arugula!)

Steps

  1. Put drained and rinsed chickpeas into a food processor with onion, celery, mayonaise, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Blend until desired chunkiness.
  2. If chickn salad is too dry, add spoonfuls of mayonaise until desired consistency.
  3. If making a sandwich, toast bread then scoop chicken salad onto one piece.
  4. Add onion slices and greens, then serve.
