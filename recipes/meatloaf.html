Meatloaf

Home

Description

This delicious all-American classic is perfect for a cozy, comforting dinner. With ground beef, breadcrumbs, and onion, this meal is easy to make and can be prepared as one large loaf or portioned into minis to be frozen and reheated throughout the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup Panko bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
    • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Combine ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, Italian season, salt, pepper, and soy sauce in a large bowl.
  3. For one large loaf, put mixture into a small loaf pan. For mini loaves, form 1/2 cup of mixture into a small loaf and place onto baking sheet lined with foil.
  4. Combine ketchup and brown sugar and apply to top of the loaf with silicone brush or spoon.
  5. Place into preheated oven. For large loaf bake for 45 minutes. For mini loaves bake for 30 minutes.
